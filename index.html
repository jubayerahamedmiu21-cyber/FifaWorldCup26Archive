About this archive

This site records the 2026 FIFA World Cup — the first held across three countries (Canada, Mexico, United States) and the first with 48 teams — as it happens, and preserves it for the future. Data lives in plain JSON files in this repo so it can be corrected, extended, and archived long after the final whistle.
